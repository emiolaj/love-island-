About Aftersun

Love Island Aftersun is the official talk-show-style companion series to Love Island USA. It's hosted by the vibrant Ariana Madix, who brings fans behind the scenes, interviews dumped islanders, and dishes out juicy recaps.

Each week, celebrity guests, ex-islanders, and superfans join Ariana to comment on the biggest moments, answer fan questions, and tease what’s coming next. Expect drama, laughs, and exclusive unseen clips.

  • Live audience energy, interactive Twitter polls, and a mini-challenge recap for dumped islanders.
  • Behind-the-scenes footage and sneak peeks of future episodes straight from the villa.
